Smart technology

Wakefield Tech uses an assisted design process based on artificial intelligence. This helps us keep costs low so we can offer a low setup fee. Once you've provided the necessary website content and design input, we go to work. 


The Design Process

  • You provide content, images, and design input
  • We run this material through an artificial intelligence design tool
  • We make improvements to the website to fit your preferences
  • We setup all the backend services, such as registering/redirecting domain names, preparing email accounts, and encrypting your site with a Secure Socket Layer (SSL) certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need 10 domain names? Isn't one enough?

One domain name is enough to get started, for sure. That's why our Basic pricing tier includes one domain name for free. However, there are benefits to having multiple domain names which point to your primary domain.

  • What happens if you already have the domain name tishomingofurnituredelivery.com but clients are having a hard time remembering that? It can be helpful to have a second domain name, like tishdelivery.com, so your potential clients can find your website a bit easier.
  • Sometimes customers aren't sure which top-level domain extension you use, especially if you have a .net or .biz domain name. If you sign up for the Business Pro account, we'll select up to 10 domain names across multiple extensions to cast the widest net possible for your customers to find.

What if I need a logo? Do you do that as well?

Our logo was produced by the system over at logojoy.com, and that's the company we'd recommend if you need a logo created. It's fast and low cost. Once you have a logo, we can help you get it added to your site. In the meantime, we can use a text-based logo using one of the beautiful fonts available to us.
